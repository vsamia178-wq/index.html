# Weekly Money Clarity Check Setup Guide ## Introduction This document provides you with a guide on how to set up your Weekly Money Clarity Check. ## Steps to Set Up 1. **Gather Your Financial Statements**: Collect bills, bank statements, and any receipts for purchases made. 2. **Choose a Time**: Set aside a specific time each week to go through your finances. 3. **Review Your Spending**: Look over your transactions from the past week and categorize them. 4. **Set Goals for the Week**: Decide on financial goals for the upcoming week based on your review. 5. **Adjust Budgets if Necessary**: Make changes to your budget based on your spending habits. ## Conclusion By regularly checking in on your finances, you can develop better spending habits and achieve your financial goals.
